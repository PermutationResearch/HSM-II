# ENTICY — Resident rules compendium (tenants & co-owners)

**Synthesis** from the Declaration of Co-ownership (English), building rules, Welcome Guide, lease addendum, and management guidelines. **Not legal advice.** When amounts or hours **conflict** across documents, say so and direct residents to **info@gestionvelora.com** for the **current** rate (DDC often prevails for ceilings; see `billing_chargeback_guide.md`).

**Emergency (water, fire, theft):** **514-777-1731** (after hours = emergency line). **Routine:** **info@gestionvelora.com**. Moves: notify management **7–15 days** ahead (email).

---

## 1. General occupancy & unit rules

- **Residential use only.** **No commercial use.** **Short-term rentals under 30 days** (e.g. Airbnb) **prohibited**.
- **Occupancy:** max **6** persons per rented unit (all ages).
- **Tenant registration** with building management within **15 days** of lease signing.
- **Insurance:** co-owners and tenants — civil liability **minimum $2,000,000**, annual proof.
- **Temperature:** unit minimum **16 °C** (61 °F).
- **Hydro-Québec & breakers:** account must stay active (AC). Do **not** turn off **main** panel breakers except emergency.
- **Locks/keys:** no add/change unit door locks without **written Board** approval; **copy of unit key** to management (emergency access).
- **Waterbeds:** prohibited.

## 2. Tranquility & noise

- **Quiet hours:** **23:00–06:00** daily.
- **Flooring:** do not expose bare concrete (soundproofing).
- **Noise reduction:** felt pads under furniture (chairs), speakers/subs on absorbing surfaces, avoid hard heels indoors.

## 3. Smoking & cannabis

- **Common areas (incl. garage, stairs, gym, pool, within ~6 m / 20 ft of entrance):** no tobacco, e-cigs, cannabis, hookahs.
- **Private balcony/loggia/terrace:** tobacco/cannabis **allowed** where applicable; **no** butts/joints thrown off balcony or into planters.
- **Cultivation:** no cannabis growing anywhere (unit or balcony).

## 4. Pets

- **One** pet per unit (dog, cat, caged bird, small rodent).
- **Common areas:** leash, carrier, or carried — never loose.
- **Prohibited:** gym, pool area, rooftop terrace.
- **Hygiene:** clean droppings immediately; **no** pet litter on balconies. **Nuisance** (barking, aggression) → removal within **two weeks** of notice.

## 5. Balconies, loggias, terraces

- **BBQ:** **propane or electric only**; **no charcoal**. Max **one** propane tank per balcony.
- **Appearance:** no hanging laundry/sheets/towels/clotheslines; no shaking rugs or throwing objects.
- **Storage:** seasonal furniture + BBQ only; **no** hot tubs, **no** enclosed solariums.
- **Plants:** no shrubs/trees **over 1.5 m**; planters not hung on/anchored to railings or glass.

## 6. Moving, deliveries, renovations

- **Notice:** **7–15 days** to management to book elevator (email **info@gestionvelora.com**).
- **Hours:** moves / large deliveries **Mon–Sat 08:00–18:00**. *(Noisy in-unit work: Mon–Fri 07:00–17:00, Sat–Sun 10:00–16:00, no holidays — per some docs.)*
- **Fees/deposits:** elevator/supervision **$250** (DDC / Welcome Guide); deposit **up to $500** (DDC) vs **$100** (Welcome Guide) vs **$250** (lease addendum) — **confirm with Velora** when booking.
- **Major work** (plumbing, electrical): **30 days** prior written notice to Board; **licensed & insured** contractors.

## 7. Waste & recycling

- **Chute:** sealed bags only, must fit; **no chute 22:00–07:00**.
- **Bulky / cardboard / glass:** ground floor (**RC**) rooms — **not** hallways or garage.

## 8. Amenities (pool, rooftop, gym)

- **Gym:** **05:00–23:00**, max **8** people, **1 guest** per unit (accompanied). Attire: no bare feet / shirtless; wipe equipment. No pets, food, alcohol, glass, loud music (headphones). **Under 16** with parent/guardian.
- **Pool & rooftop:** typically **08:00–21:00** (confirm signs); **shower before pool**; **2 guests** max (accompanied). Rooftop capacity **30** people. No glass, food, alcohol, smoking/vaping, loud music, pets in pool/rooftop. **Under 16** with adult. BBQ on roof only if common equipment, cleaned after use.
- **Registration:** QR code at elevator / terrace door where applicable.

## 9. Parking & lockers

- **Parking:** **1** vehicle in good order **or** **2** motorcycles per space; **no** washing/repairs in garage; **no** studded tires; **no** personal storage in spaces.
- **Lockers:** own padlock; **no** flammables, hazardous material, **propane**; **no** commercial subletting of locker.

## 10. Common areas & deliveries

- **Expedibox:** parcels only — **no** personal storage, **no** keys (incl. short-term rental keys); misuse → fines / surveillance.
- **Hallways:** no boots/shoes/strollers/bikes outside doors; **no** bikes/skateboards indoors.

## 11. Fines & penalties (summary)

- **Progressive fines:** 1st **$100**, 2nd **$150**, then **+$50** per subsequent notice (same fiscal year framing per docs).
- **Ongoing violations** (e.g. illegal work, illegal STR): **$150** on 2nd-notice day + **$50/day** continuing.
- **Joint liability:** **co-owner (landlord)** liable for tenant/guest fines and damages; syndicate bills **co-owner** account.

**Full fee tables & Velora contract vs syndicate chargebacks:** see **`billing_chargeback_guide.md`**.

## 12. Access, intercom, Expedibox (operational)

- **Intercom:** Canadian phone number; display **First initial + Last name**; visitor call → press **9** to unlock.
- **Fob / remote replacement (Welcome Guide):** fob **$50**; garage remote **$100** (DDC may differ on older fob price — prefer current guide).
- **Spare key** mandatory for management (emergency); forced entry costs → co-owner if no key.

## 13. In-unit maintenance & utilities (highlights)

- **Central AC** for cooling; winter heating per wall units + ventilation; **DKN Cloud NA** app where applicable; **do not** shut breaker for AC/heat system.
- **PRV:** co-owner responsible for **annual** plumbing pressure relief valve adjustment (per guidelines).
- **Smoke alarms:** tenant maintenance; **no** tampering/disabling.
- **Windows:** syndicate washes **inaccessible** exterior glass; residents clean **patio doors** and **interior balcony glass** they can safely reach.

## 14. Payments & tenant file (co-owners)

- **Condo fees:** PAD 1st of month or **12** post-dated cheques to **SDC Enticy** (per office instructions).
- **Tenant file to syndicate:** lease, **$2M** proof, **signed building rules**, emergency contacts (and vehicle info where required).

## 15. Unit maintenance, hygiene, safety (addendum-style)

- Proper use of toilets/plumbing; blockages → repair cost to responsible party.
- **Mold/pests:** tenant obligation to maintain unit; infestations addressed per lease/rules.
- **Noxious odors / hazardous materials** not allowed.
- **Fire stairs:** emergency use only.
- **Inspection access:** tenants allow authorized personnel with **prior notice** (except emergency).

## 16. Appliances & end of lease

- **Washers/dryers** only with **landlord** prior written consent; heavy appliances restricted to avoid overload.
- **Restoration** at lease end to original condition, **reasonable wear and tear** excepted.

## 17. Aesthetics & window treatments

- **No** sheets/newspaper/clothing/paper as curtains (temp or permanent).
- **Exterior-visible** treatments must meet **Board** visual standards.
- **Balconies:** no enclosure (solarium); **no** screens, awnings, pergolas on balcony/loggia/terrace (per declaration addendum themes).

## 18. Storage & subletting

- Lockers **personal use only**; **no** commercial rental to public.
- **Sublease / assignment** must comply with **DDC** policies.

## 19. Tenant financial liability & eviction (summary)

- Tenant liable for repair costs from their breach, unlawful acts, etc. (per addendum); **syndicate still bills co-owner** — landlord recovers privately.
- **Serious disturbance** or **illegal activity** → grounds for lease termination / eviction process; syndicate may seek TAL termination with **notice to landlord and tenant**.
