# ENTICY — Billing & chargeback guide (structured)

**Sources:** 2025 management contract (Velora Immobilier Inc.), English DDC, 2025–2026 lease amendment, Welcome Guide. **Not legal advice.**

**Two different money flows:**

- **A)** What **Velora (property management)** bills **to the syndicate / co-owners collectively** under the **management contract**.
- **B)** What the **syndicate** charges **back to an individual co-owner’s account** under the **DDC and building rules** (always the co-owner, even if a tenant caused the issue).

---

## A. Velora management contract billing (what the PM charges the syndicate)

Per **2025 Management Contract**, **Velora Immobilier Inc.** may charge:

- **Standard management fee:** **$33** per residential unit per month.
- **Emergency after-hours interventions:** outside normal hours (**8:00 p.m.–6:00 a.m.** weekdays; **6:00 p.m.–8:00 a.m.** weekends): **$60/hour** (main Manager), **$40/hour** (Assistant/Coordinator).
- **Additional services (BoD approval required):** tasks outside the standard contract (e.g. supervising voluntary major renovations, organizing special general meetings): **$50/hour** (Manager), **$25/hour** (Assistant).
- **Notary / sales questionnaires:** Velora may perform the administrative work for sales and charge the **transferring co-owner** at **DDC fixed rates** (typically **$675** total for the usual bundle) **without markup** beyond what the DDC authorizes.
- **Translations:** if the **syndicate** requests translations → additional service billed per contract. If a **specific co-owner** requests a translation → billed **directly to that co-owner**.
- **Private loss management:** if a co-owner asks Velora to handle a **private** insurance claim (loss **inside their unit**, not a syndicate claim), Velora bills that co-owner **directly**; **separate** from syndicate billing.

## B. Syndicate chargebacks & penalties to co-owners (DDC & rules)

Under the **DDC and building rules**, the syndicate bills **penalties, fees, and damages to the co-owner’s account**, whether the fault is the co-owner’s or a **tenant’s**.

### B1. Staff & management interventions (co-owner / tenant fault)

- **Management intervention** (manager must act due to co-owner or tenant **default**): **$150/hour**, **minimum 3 hours = $450**.
- **Concierge / handyman intervention:** **$100/hour**.
- **Clean-up fee:** **$100/hour** (e.g. garbage spill in hallways during transport).
- **Forced entry / alarm disable:** if management must force a door or disable an alarm because no spare key/code was provided → **repair costs and emergency dispatch** charged fully to the **co-owner**.

### B2. Rule violations (fines)

- **Standard infractions:** 1st notice **$100**; 2nd **$150**; each subsequent notice **+$50** (3rd **$200**, 4th **$250**, etc.).
- **Ongoing violations** (e.g. illegal renovations, illegal short-term rentals): **$150** on the day the **2nd notice** is received, plus **$50 per day** for each day the violation **continues**.
- **Legal fees:** extrajudicial costs (e.g. lawyer) incurred by the syndicate to **force compliance** → charged to the **co-owner**.

### B3. Moving & delivery fees

- **Supervision / elevator fee:** **$250** non-refundable per DDC and Welcome Guide. *(2025–2026 lease amendment may cite **$150**; **DDC generally prevails**—confirm active rate with management.)*
- **Damage deposit:** DDC authorizes up to **$500**. *(Welcome Guide **$100**; lease amendment **$250**—management clarifies active deposit; **legal ceiling $500**.)*

### B4. Access devices & replacements

- **Electronic fob (puce):** **$50** each (updated Welcome Guide). *(Original DDC excerpt may list **$20**—prefer current guide unless BoD directs otherwise.)*
- **Garage remote (manette):** **$100** each.
- **Garage access ramp handle:** flat replacement **$30**.

### B5. Real estate transactions (DRCOP)

- Statement of common charges: **$225**
- General information / documents: **$225**
- Certificate of condition: **$225**
- **Total typical package: $675** (billed to **seller**).
- Consultation of condominium registers: **$50/hour**.

### B6. Financial defaults (late condo fees)

- **Late payment / NSF cheque:** **$100** penalty **each** delayed payment or bounced cheque.
- **Interest:** **2% per month** (**26.82%** annually) on arrears.
- **Loss of benefit of term:** if a payment is **more than 7 days** late, the Board may demand immediate payment of the **entire remaining annual balance** of that co-owner’s condo fees (per DDC).

## Quick distinction for staff

| Situation | Bucket |
|-----------|--------|
| Monthly per-unit fee, after-hours syndicate emergency coverage rates, BoD-approved extra PM hours | **A — Management contract** |
| Fines, DDC intervention $150/h (min 3 h), concierge $100/h, move fees, fobs/DRCOP per DDC, late fees | **B — Syndicate → co-owner** |

If unclear, **escalate to PM / treasurer** before quoting a final amount.
