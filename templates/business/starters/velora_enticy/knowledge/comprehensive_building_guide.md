# ENTICY — Comprehensive resident & operations guide (EN + legal FR summary)

**Purpose:** Operational rules for **Syndicat ENTICY (TCS ENTICY)** from Welcome Guide, Lease Addendum, English/French DDC excerpts, **2025 management contract**, and Velora Monday.com playbooks. **Complements** `integrated_governing_docs.md` (numbered source refs). **Billing (PM vs syndicate chargebacks):** see **`billing_chargeback_guide.md`** (parts A and B). **Precedence:** If a dollar amount or rule **conflicts** between documents, state the conflict and tell the resident to confirm with **info@gestionvelora.com**—do not pick a number silently.

**Contacts:** Non-emergency **info@gestionvelora.com**. Emergency line **514-777-1731** (after 5 p.m. = emergencies only: water, fire, theft). Office visits by appointment. Phone 9 a.m.–5 p.m. for non-emergency direct calls where applicable.

## 1. General occupancy & unit rules

- **Residential use only.** **Short-term rentals under 30 days (e.g. Airbnb) are prohibited.**
- **Occupancy (rented units):** max **6** people regardless of age.
- **Tenant registration:** with building management within **15 days** of lease signing (landlord notifies syndicate per DDC).
- **Insurance:** co-owners and tenants — civil liability **minimum $2,000,000**; annual proof required.
- **Temperature:** maintain unit at least **16 °C (61 °F)**.
- **Hydro-Québec / breakers:** account must stay active for central AC; do **not** turn off main panel breakers except emergency.
- **Locks / keys:** no adding/changing unit door locks without **written Board** approval; **copy of unit key to management** mandatory (emergency access).
- **Waterbeds:** prohibited.

---

## 2. Tranquility & noise

- **Quiet hours:** **11 p.m.–6 a.m.** daily.
- **Soundproofing:** do not modify floors to expose bare concrete.
- **Mitigation:** felt pads under furniture (especially chairs); speakers/subwoofers on absorbing cushions; avoid hard-heeled shoes indoors.

---

## 3. Smoking & cannabis

- **Common areas:** tobacco, e-cigarettes, cannabis, hookahs **prohibited** indoors and outdoors in common areas, including garage, stairs, gym, pool, and **within ~20 ft (6 m) of building entrance**.
- **Private balconies/loggias/terraces:** smoking tobacco/cannabis **allowed**; **no** throwing butts/joints off balcony or into planters (fire risk).
- **Cultivation:** cannabis growing **prohibited** everywhere (units and balconies).

---

## 4. Pets

- **One pet per unit** (dog, cat, caged bird, small rodent).
- **Common areas:** never loose; leash, carrier, or in arms.
- **Banned:** gym, pool area, rooftop terrace.
- **Hygiene:** clean droppings immediately; **no** pet litter on balconies. Nuisance pets (e.g. constant barking, aggression): removal within **two weeks** of notice.

---

## 5. Balconies, loggias & terraces

- **BBQ:** propane or electric only; **one** propane tank per balcony; **charcoal forbidden**.
- **Appearance:** no laundry/lines; no shaking rugs/mops or throwing objects off balcony.
- **Snow:** copropriétaire with exclusive use must clear snow from balcony/terrace where applicable.
- **Storage / structures:** seasonal furniture + BBQ only; **no** hot tubs, enclosed solariums, **no** enclosing balconies; **no** screens, awnings, or pergolas unless Board standards say otherwise.
- **Plants:** no shrubs/trees **over 1.5 m**; planters **not** hung/anchored to railings or glass screens.

---

## 6. Moving, deliveries & renovations

- **Notice:** email **info@gestionvelora.com** **7–15 days** ahead to book elevator / large delivery; management must confirm.
- **Elevator:** **no** blocking/using elevator for furniture **without prior written authorization**.
- **Hours (moves / large deliveries):** Mon–Sat **8 a.m.–6 p.m.**
- **Noisy in-unit work:** Mon–Fri **7 a.m.–5 p.m.**; Sat–Sun **10 a.m.–4 p.m.**; **not** on holidays.
- **Fees / deposits (DISCREPANCY):** DDC / playbook often cite **$250** supervision + **$500** damage deposit; Welcome Guide mentions **$250** fee + **$100** deposit; Lease Addendum may cite **$150** + **$250** deposit. **Always:** confirm active rate with management when booking.
- **Major renovations:** **30 days** prior written notice to Board; licensed insured contractors for plumbing/electrical per DDC.

---

## 7. Waste & recycling

- **Chute:** only sealed household bags that fit easily; **no chute 10 p.m.–7 a.m.** No loose food, heavy objects, construction waste, hazardous waste, cardboard, furniture, styrofoam in chute.
- **Large items / recycling:** ground floor (**RC**) rooms; flatten cardboard; bulky items to designated areas.
- **Common areas / garage:** no leaving garbage, recycling, or bulky items; soiling common areas → cleaning fees + possible penalties.

## 8. Amenities — gym, pool, rooftop

**Gym**

- **Hours:** **5 a.m.–11 p.m.** (no access outside hours without Board written approval).
- **Capacity:** **8** people; **1 guest per unit**, guest with resident at all times.
- **Attire:** no shirtless / barefoot; wipe equipment after use; headphones only (no loud speakers).
- **Prohibited:** pets, commercial use, food, alcohol, glass.
- **Minors under 16:** accompanied by parent/guardian.

**Pool & rooftop**

- **Hours:** typically **8 a.m.–9 p.m.** daily (some docs say pool to 8 p.m.—confirm if needed). **Closed midnight–7 a.m.**
- **Registration:** QR scan (elevator screen / terrace door) where required.
- **Rooftop capacity:** **30** people max.
- **Guests:** **2** max per unit at pool/rooftop, accompanied at all times.
- **Pool:** shower before entering; appropriate swimwear; no swimming impaired; stay within demarcated rooftop perimeter (no roof access beyond).
- **Prohibited:** glass, food, alcohol, smoking/vaping, pets on rooftop/pool, loud music. Minors under 16 with adult supervision; no pool toys/water guns (floats may be allowed per rules).
- **Rooftop BBQs:** common equipment allowed if cleaned after use.

## 9. Parking & lockers

- **Per space:** **one** car **or** **two** motorcycles in good order. **No** car washing or mechanical repairs in garage; **studded tires forbidden**.
- **No storage** of personal items in parking space.
- **Lockers:** resident’s padlock; **no** flammable liquids or hazardous materials; **no** propane in lockers; **no commercial subletting** of lockers.
- **Garage door handle (indoor ramp access):** fixed replacement fee **$30** if applicable per fee schedule.

## 10. Common areas, Expedibox, intercom

- **Expedibox:** parcels only; **no** personal storage or keys (including Airbnb keys); monitored; misuse → fines.
- **Hallways:** no boots/shoes/strollers/bikes outside doors; no bikes/skateboards indoors.
- **Advertising:** no physical “for rent/for sale” signs in mailroom/common areas; online only.
- **Intercom:** **Canadian** phone number required; display = **First initial + Last name**; visitor call → press **9** to unlock.
- **Fobs / remotes (DISCREPANCY):** DDC excerpt lists fob **$20**; Welcome Guide **$50** per fob; garage remote **$100**. State both + confirm with management.

## 11. Fines & rule violations

- **Progressive (typical per fiscal year):** 1st notice **$100**; 2nd **$150**; each further notice **+$50** (3rd $200, etc.).
- **Ongoing violations:** per authoritative schedule → **$150** on the day of the **2nd notice** + **$50/day** while violation continues (see **`billing_chargeback_guide.md` § B2**). If another document suggests only a flat daily amount, **flag discrepancy** to management.
- **Financial responsibility:** fines and syndicate intervention costs go to **co-owner**; landlord jointly liable for tenant/guest acts.

## 13. In-unit maintenance, utilities & safety

- **AC / heating:** central AC for **cooling**; winter use **electric wall heaters** + ventilation; **DKN Cloud NA** app for control; **do not** turn off AC/heating breaker.
- **PRV:** owner **annual** adjustment of plumbing pressure relief valve.
- **Smoke alarms:** tenant maintains; **no** tampering/disabling.
- **Windows:** syndicate washes **inaccessible** exterior windows annually (~spring/summer); residents clean **patio doors** and **interior side of balcony/terrace glass**.
- **Wall AC filters & shared ducts:** **syndicate** maintains per building-specific DDC (shared compressors).
- **Air exchanger filter:** co-owner changes **≥1×/year**.
- **Plumbing:** proper toilet/fixture use; blockages from misuse → charged to responsible party.
- **Mold / pests:** tenant obligation to maintain unit to prevent mold; pest-free environment.
- **Noxious odors / hazards:** residents must prevent.
- **Fire stairs:** emergency use only (not routine).
- **Inspection access:** tenants allow authorized personnel with proper notice except emergency.
- **Washers/dryers:** not without **landlord written consent**; heavy appliances restricted to avoid overload.
- **End of lease:** restore to original condition accounting for normal wear.
- **Window coverings:** no sheets/newspaper/clothes/paper as curtains; exterior-visible treatments per **Board** harmony standards.

## 14. Co-owner legal points (often misunderstood) — FR sources

- **Access to private portion:** per DDC (e.g. **3.6.3**, **15.1.4**): administrators/manager may access private portions at reasonable hours with **≥24 h written notice**, except emergency. **Mandatory** spare key (and alarm code if any) to Board; forced entry costs if no key → co-owner liable. No lock changes without written CA approval.
- **Voting:** votes proportional to **relative fraction value** (e.g. **9.3**, **16.5.15**), not one unit one vote.
- **Loss of vote:** **>3 months** arrears on condo fees → may lose vote/speech at AGM until balance paid (**16.5.16** style).
- **Parking/locker rental:** cannot rent to **outsiders**—only to other residents/co-owners/tenants of building or syndicate (**6.4.3.2**, **13.2.5** style); cannot sell parking interest separately from unit.
- **Time-share / multipropriété:** **13.1.3** style—no periodic successive use structure for a fraction.
- **Buyer liability for seller arrears:** buyer can be **jointly liable** for seller’s unpaid condo fees; **not** if syndicate fails to provide statement within **15 days** when requested—buyer should get état des charges before closing (**17.2.x** family).

## 15. Building-specific structural / transitional provisions

- **Phased building:** lower section active; upper floors may be **transitional lot** with ongoing work—**construction easement**: co-owners must tolerate noise/dust from declarant work; **no strict deadline** to finish in materials provided.
- **Special irrevocable mandate:** purchasers may grant declarant/rep **proxy** for certain votes (cadastre, easements, transitional lot) for **two years after completion of all construction** (verify exact text).
- **Historic façade (Mackay / René-Lévesque):** maintenance/repair cost = **common expense** for **all** co-owners by relative value, in perpetuity.
- **Unifying two units:** contiguous units may be unified with **Board written consent**; opening in common wall allowed if reversible per DDC.

---

## 16. Landlord vs tenant vs syndicate / management

- **Lease relationship:** between **co-owner** and **tenant**; management **does not** manage the lease.
- **Co-owner must:** maintain private unit, give tenant **by-laws**, register tenant in **15 days**, provide insurance proof to syndicate.
- **Billing:** syndicate charges **co-owner** only for fines, interventions, damages—not tenant directly. Co-owner recovers from tenant privately; lease addendum may assign tenant liability for their breaches.
- **In-unit repairs:** generally **landlord**; tenant pays for damage from their fault (addendum). Common elements / mandatory syndicate work → appropriate syndicate/management channels.
- **Severe misconduct / TAL:** syndicate may seek lease termination for serious prejudice after formal notice to **both** landlord and tenant.

**Tenants** must comply with municipal, provincial, federal law, **Civil Code of Québec**, and **TAL** rules where applicable.

## 17. Monday.com & operational playbooks

**Forms / boards (names as used internally)**

- **New Occupant Onboarding** — lease, signed rules, emergency contacts, vehicle info; reminders if missing by move-in.
- **Intercom & Expedibox Setup** — Canadian phone; Expedibox needs tenant email.

- **Property Sales & Transfers** — anticipated/actual closing; checklist; DRCOP fees to seller.
- **Building Maintenance & Technical Log** — preventive maintenance (Frequency, Last Service, Next Due, PM reminders ~14 days); **Ad-hoc Technical Requests** for handyman path.
- **Project Financial Lifecycle & BoD Approval** — vendor invoices: upload PDF to **Invoice Received (Files)**, **Invoice Amount**, **Invoice Due Date**, status **Invoice Received - Pending Payment** → notifies treasurer/AP; paid → **Payment Processed** + proof.

## 18. Scenario quick routing

**Tenant: something broken inside unit**

- Private portion maintenance → **landlord** first (DDC **15.1.11** style).
- Tenant fault / addendum rules → tenant may owe landlord.
- Common portion or building system → **Co-owner Inquiries** / **Resident Complaints** or **Building Maintenance & Technical Log** (Ad-hoc).

**Vendor invoice for syndicate**

- **Project Financial Lifecycle** board; columns as above; status triggers payment workflow.

**Chargeback to co-owner**

- Pay vendor via lifecycle board; accounting charges co-owner per **`billing_chargeback_guide.md` § B** (syndicate → co-owner: **$150/h** min **3 h**, concierge/clean **$100/h**, etc.). Do **not** confuse with **§ A** (contract rates the syndicate pays Velora, e.g. **$33/unit/mo**, after-hours **$60/$40**).
