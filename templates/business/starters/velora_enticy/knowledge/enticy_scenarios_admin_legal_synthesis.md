## 1. Scenario: breakage inside a unit (tenant request)

**Situation:** Tenant reports something broken **inside** the unit and wants syndic/management to repair.

- **DDC (private portion):** Co-owner must maintain private portion and accessories **at own expense** (e.g. §15.1.11 style provisions in English DDC).
- **Tenant (addendum):** Tenant responsible for costs from **their** violations; maintain unit to prevent mold/pests (rules referenced in addendum).
- **Velora handling:**
  - **Private portion** → direct tenant to **landlord** for repairs.
  - **Common portion** (e.g. element in wall serving building, cracked exterior window per DDC split) or **general building request** → **Co-owner Inquiries** / **Resident Complaints** (Monday.com) as applicable.
  - If handyman/syndicate intervention → **Building Maintenance & Technical Log (Carnet d’Entretien)** → **Ad-hoc Technical Requests** group.

## 4. DDC aspects often unknown to co-owners (FR summary)

1. **Access & keys:** Administrators/manager may access private portions at **reasonable** times with **≥24 h written notice**, except **urgency**. **Mandatory spare key** to syndic; forced-entry cost if absent; **no** lock change without written CA authorization (e.g. arts. 3.6.3, 15.1.4 themes).
2. **Votes:** Votes **proportional to relative value** of fraction — not “one condo one vote” (e.g. 9.3, 16.5.15 themes).
3. **Loss of vote:** **more than 3 months** arrears on condo fees → **no vote** at AG (may attend but not speak/vote until paid) (e.g. 16.5.16).
4. **Financial severity:** **Loss of benefit of term** — **7 days** late may trigger demand for **full remaining annual** charges (e.g. 17.2.8). **Legal hypothec** after **30 days** arrears; legal costs to register/radiate → co-owner (e.g. 17.2.12).
5. **Parking/locker rental:** Spaces/lockers **not** rented to **outsiders** — only to **residents** of the building or syndic (e.g. 6.4.3.2, 13.2.5 themes). No selling “parking only” separate from unit transfer.
6. **Multipropriété / time-share:** **Prohibited** to hold a fraction for successive periodic use by multiple parties (e.g. 13.1.3).

## 5. Structural & governance specifics (ENTICY)

- **Phased building / transitional lot:** Lower section occupied; upper floors may be **transitional lot** with **ongoing work**. **Construction easement** — co-owners must **tolerate** noise/dust from work above; **declarant** not strictly bound to a finish date in the excerpted structure.
- **Special irrevocable mandate:** Purchasers may consent to mandate for **declarant/rep** to vote on **cadastral / easement / transitional lot** matters until **two years after completion of all construction**.
- **Historic façade** (René-Lévesque / Mackay): maintenance/repair cost = **common expense** for **all** co-owners (relative value), **not** only line-of-façade units.
- **Maintenance splits (exceptions):** Syndicate maintains **wall-mounted AC filters** and **shared ventilation** paths where one unit affects others; co-owner changes **air exchanger** filter ≥1×/year. Window washing split per governing docs (inaccessible exterior vs balcony-accessible glass).
- **Unifying two adjacent units:** Possible with **prior written BoD** authorization; opening in **common** dividing wall may be **reversible**.

## 6. Purchase — prior owner arrears

- Buyer may be **jointly liable** with seller for **unpaid condo fees** at sale. **Protection:** notary requests **statement of charges** from syndic; if syndic does **not** respond within **15 days**, buyer may not be bound to seller’s debt (verify current Civil Code / practice with counsel).

## 9. Syndicate vs landlord vs tenant (binding split for answers)

- **Syndicate/management do not manage the lease** or act as landlord.
- **Co-owner–landlord:** unit security, maintenance standards, **enforce** building rules with tenant, **register** tenant with syndic within **15 days**, provide rules + insurance proof.
- **Financial:** syndicate **never** invoices tenant directly for syndicate charges; **co-owner account** debited; landlord recovers from tenant **privately** per lease (management does not collect from renter).
- **Management talks to tenant** for **building operations** (e.g. mandatory work through unit) and **extreme** cases leading to lease termination, with **due notice** to **both** landlord and tenant.

## 10. Law compliance (tenant)

Tenants must obey **municipal, provincial, federal** law including **Civil Code of Québec** and **TAL** rules where applicable. **Fines/charges** still flow to **co-owner** account; **chargeback to tenant** is **private** landlord–tenant matter unless lease/addendum assigns it.
